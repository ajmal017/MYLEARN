This is the strategy suggested to me by an influential stock broker from Mumbai:

I don’t believe in writing long-form content and therefore will make this short and to the point. Here’s the 2-2-2 Strategy.

STEP 1: Slot Your Trading Style

What are you?

  1. Day trader
  2. Weekly trader
  3. Short term trader (1-2 months)
  4. Medium term trader (6-12 months)
  5. Long term value investor

This strategy does not apply to medium term and long term investors.

Step 2: The 2-2-2 Stock Market Trading Strategy

For Day Traders — If a trade does not swing your way in 2 HOURS, get out of it

For Weekly or Fortnightly Traders — If a trade does not swing your way in 2 DAYS, get out of it

For Short Term Traders — If a trade does not swing your way in 2 WEEKS, get out of it

The broker I met says this strategy works 80% of the time.

Try it for a couple of trades. If it works for you, great!

That’s it.
