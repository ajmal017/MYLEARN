Financial Risk Calculator

  1. Do not insert commas in figures. For example, write 1 crore as 10000000 NOT 1,00,00,000
  2. For negative figures, use (hyphen)(figure). For example, if Working Capital is negative 1 crore, write it as -10000000
  3. Be consistent with figures. For eg, if you are writing 10 crores as 10 then 50 crores should be written as 50, 2 crores as 2 and 50 lakhs as 0.50
