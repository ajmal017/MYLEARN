Shree Vasu Logistics Review

Posted By: sunil.tinani May 20, 2018

Shree Vasu Logistics Review
Rate this post

There are many logistics companies listed on the NSE and BSE SME boards. Does Shree Vau Logistics make the cut?

Read on to find out:

MOAT:

To pun, there are truckloads of logistics companies out there and Shree Vasu is just another (it is based in Chattisgarh). However, the company claims to own temperature controlled, modern, safe and hygienic warehouses (850000 sft).

It also claims to be the largest conglomerate in CFA & LOGISTICS business in Central & Eastern India. These claims have not been verified for this review.

The company’s website boasts of leading clients such as Dabur, Marico, Parle, Godrej, etc. However, these companies are so huge that they would be doing business with most organized logistic firms. The company too acknowledges the fact in the prospectus that the market is fiercely competitive. Moreover, all the company’s warehouses are situated in Chattisgarh.

It is safe to say there is no moat.

SUSPENSE ASSOCIATED COMPANIES AND PROMOTER’S RELATIONS:

Actually this is a first – the company wrote in December 2017 and February 2018 to the promoters and their relatives to disclose all their associated concerns/relations. There was no reply from many till the filing of the prospectus in March 2018, and therefore such companies/people have not been disclosed in the prospectus. This is a big red flag.

IN-HOUSE COMPETITION

Several group companies belonging to the promoter group are freely competing with Shree Vasu Logistics. This is another red flag.

CONCLUSION

At this point I don’t want to go any further or bother about the finances. This is because

a. I am concerned that group entities and relatives have not been fully accounted for in the prospectus. Are they taking salaries or commissions from the company? Are they operating competing firms? There are no answers to these questions.

b. Promoters and group entities competing with the issuer company in a market that is hugely competitive, and that too in one state dilutes whatever advantage the company claims on its website.

I would AVOID this bumpy long and winding road issue. Those who are yet interested can read through the rest of the prospectus and prospect for nuggets and state their research in the comments below.
