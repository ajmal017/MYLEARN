DeBock Sales and Marketing IPO Review

DeBock Sales and Marketing IPO Review
4.3 (86.67%) 3 votes

Jaipur-based Debock Sales and Marketing, which is engaged in manufacturing and trading of agricultural equipment and in hospitality services, is issuing 22.20 lakh shares at Rs 20.

Is the IPO worth applying? Find out in our unbiased review:

MOAT:

The company owns a hotel, Hotel Debock Inn, in Tonk, Jaipur.

It sells agricultural equipment manufactured by the promoter’s proprietorship firm, Eagle Sales.

The company is also into sales and marketing. I checked their website and it is not mentioned what the company is marketing.

The company has no moat to speak of.

COMPLIANCE ISSUES:

The company has been negligent in complying with statutory flings.

CRIMINAL CASES AGAINST THE PROMOTERS:

There are 4 criminal cases against the promoter/directors. Also, the promoter has changed his name from Mukesh Kumar Mahawar to Mukesh Manveer Singh. Could it be because of these cases?

GROUP COMPANIES’ LOSSES

Other companies associated with the group are in losses.

CONCLUSION

I have by now read enough to form an opinion, which is – AVOID.
