Porinju Veliyath is a master stock picker and has developed a huge cult following within a short span of time

I am his fan as well and cling on to every word he says and try to read between the lines, as far as I can.

His tweets and comments on TV send investors into a stock-buying frenzy with recent examples of binge-buying being Shalimar Paints, Orient Bell and Mirza International.

This is my first blog post and you can say that it is like a tribute to Porinju’s stock picking genius.

Without much ado, here is a screenshot of Prinju’s personal portfolio as on 31 March 2017.

 

As you can see, Porinju has:

EXITED

1. BDH Industries
2. Emkay Global

REDUCED

  1. Izmo
  2. Balkrishna Paper Mills (over Sep 2016)

INCREASED

  1. Sarda Plywood

Before analysing Porinju’s portfolio, know this:

  1. That this portfolio is as on 31 March, and that one day, one single moment in the stock market moves at the speed of sound. So it is possible that Porinju has shuffled his portfolio dramatically since then.
  2. I agree with his decision to reduce Izmo because after going through the company’s website it does seem that their business model has not kept pace with the changes on the ground.
  3. Do not act on this blog post. It is mere information. I suggest you contact Porinju on his Twitter handle and chat with him before making any decision. In any case, he, or any analyst for that matter, will not give you tips, so it’s up to you.
  4. All the information published here is in public domain, all you have to do is search for it.
