You probably have heard about Rakesh Jhunjhunwala, Porinju Veliyath, Ramesh Damani, Dolly Khanna and a bunch of other legendary investors and stock pickers in Indian stock market.

These guys always hog the limelight and are as famous as film stars to the Indian investing community.

BUT.. there are others too. These investors are as significant as the main dudes, but they are lesser-known.

Won’t you stand to benefit if you could track these investors’ holding PLUS learn what they are buying or selling in the markets?

Of course, you sure would.

And I’m going to show you how to do that:

To know the holding of any of the Indian Stock market Legends, go to RATESTAR, and feed in the name in the Investor’s Tab.

To Figure out what they are buying or selling on an everyday basis, head to the following Disclosures section of BSE

BSE Insider Trading
BSE SAST Disclosures
BSE and NSE Bulk Deals

To figure out what is being said about them and their investment in the news or on blogs, set up a Google Alert per investor name. You’ll need a Gmail account to do this.
